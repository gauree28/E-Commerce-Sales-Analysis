KPIs Created:
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Category-wise Revenue
- Top Products

DAX Measures:
Total Revenue = SUMX(ecommerce_sales, ecommerce_sales[quantity] * ecommerce_sales[price])
Total Profit = SUM(ecommerce_sales[profit])

Visuals Used:
- Bar Chart
- Line Chart
- KPI Cards
- Slicers (Category, Month)
